Transformer Networks

Transformers are deep learning models introduced in 2017 that revolutionized NLP tasks with self-attention mechanisms.

Model Structure

Key components include encoder-decoder architecture, multi-head self-attention, and positional encoding.

Applications

Transformers power models like BERT, GPT, and are used in translation, summarization, and image tasks.
